About Us

Baby and Me is our innovative solution to expectant mothers limited access to medical services in Uganda.


We are a team of web developers, building our skills as students of Outbox EDU Full-Stack Online Bootcamp. Our project is supervised by our Learning Facilitator Roland Sankara.

OUR VISION

Unlimited access to affordable, quality medical services and health care for all expectant mothers.

OUR MISSION

Closing the gap between affordable, quality medical services and health-care for expectant mothers.

OUR VALUES

Integrity, Passion, Empathy, Excellence, and Reliably delivering on our promises to close the gap.
